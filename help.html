Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new project?
To create a new project, click the "New Project" button in the top right corner of the dashboard. Fill in the project details including name, description, start date, and team members. Click "Create" to save your project.
How do I assign resources to a project?
Navigate to the Resource Management module, select your project, and click "Assign Resources". You can search for team members by name or skills, and the AI will suggest the best matches based on availability and expertise.
How do I track time on projects?
Go to the Time Tracking module and select your project. You can log time manually or use the timer feature. Time entries are automatically synced with your timesheet and can be submitted for approval.
How do I generate reports?
Visit the Reports & Analytics module and choose from 50+ pre-built templates. You can customize reports, schedule automatic generation, and export to PDF or Excel format.
What AI features are available?
Our platform includes 40+ AI systems including resource matching (92% accuracy), risk prediction (87% accuracy), budget forecasting (95% accuracy), and automated insights across all modules.
